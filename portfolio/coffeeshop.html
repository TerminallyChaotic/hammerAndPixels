Our Story

Brewed with
intention.

Groundwork started with a simple idea: Simsbury deserved a coffee shop that cared as much about community as it did about the coffee. We source our beans from small-lot roasters, bake our pastries fresh every morning, and keep the WiFi strong.

Whether you're settling in for a work session, catching up with friends, or just need a perfect cortado on your way to the trail, we're glad you're here.
