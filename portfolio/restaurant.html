Our Story

Rooted in fire.
Refined by hand.

Embers & Oak was born from a simple conviction: the best meals happen when great ingredients meet open flame. Nestled in Hartford's historic district, our kitchen pays homage to primal cooking techniques while embracing the precision of contemporary cuisine.

Every dish tells a story of Connecticut's seasons -- from spring morels foraged in Litchfield County to winter root vegetables from farms in the valley. We work directly with local growers, fishermen, and artisans to bring the region's finest to your table.

The Chef

Elena Morales

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

With fifteen years in kitchens from New York to Barcelona, Chef Morales brings a philosophy rooted in simplicity and respect for ingredients. Her time at Mugaritz and Gramercy Tavern shaped a style that balances technical precision with rustic warmth.

At Embers & Oak, Elena oversees every aspect of the culinary program, from sourcing to plating. Her commitment to Connecticut's agricultural community drives the restaurant's farm-forward identity.

"The fire doesn't lie. It demands your attention and rewards your patience. That's what we try to bring to every plate."

Chef Elena Morales at the open hearth
