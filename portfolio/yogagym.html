Our Philosophy

Strength through stillness.
Growth through movement.

Root & Rise was built on the idea that wellness isn't one-size-fits-all. Whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or training for your hundredth race, our classes are designed to challenge, restore, and transform.

We blend traditional yoga philosophy with modern fitness science, offering a range of classes from restorative yin to high-intensity strength training. Our 3,500 sq ft studio features heated and non-heated rooms, a meditation alcove, and a small retail space featuring local wellness brands.
