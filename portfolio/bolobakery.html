Our Story

A little bakery
with a lot of heart.

Bolo Bakery & Café is Plainville's home for Portuguese-inspired baked goods and fresh breakfast. We bake everything from scratch every morning using quality ingredients and time-tested recipes.

Whether you're grabbing a pastel de nata on your way to work, sitting down for fluffy pancakes, or picking up a dozen pastries for the office — we're here to make your day a little sweeter.
