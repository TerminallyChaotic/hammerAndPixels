Our Story

A family tradition
since 1996.

Mangiafico's Pastry Shoppe has been a staple in the heart of Plainville for nearly three decades. What started as a family dream has grown into the neighborhood's go-to destination for authentic Italian pastries, custom celebration cakes, and the kind of baked goods that bring people together.

Every item is made from scratch using recipes passed down through generations. From our signature cannoli with house-made ricotta filling to our buttery Italian cookies, we put the same care into every pastry that our family has for decades.
